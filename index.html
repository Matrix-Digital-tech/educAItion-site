AI is changing our future.
Together, we can shape it.

educAItion is a hands-on AI literacy camp where kids ages 8–12 and their parents explore what AI is, how it works, and their role in shaping its future. Five days of exploration, creation, and critical thinking for the whole family.

Register for the Pilot

What You'll All Experience

Not screen time. Not a coding bootcamp. A week of guided discovery that leaves kids curious, creative, and critical thinkers — and parents inspired by what their kids can do.

Explore AI Deeply

Kids and parents explore what AI actually is through hands-on activities — not lectures. They'll train simple models, see bias in action, and discover the science behind the headlines. Together.

Build Something Real

Every kid picks a problem they care about on Day 1 and builds a solution alongside a group — working together all week long. On Day 5, they present their creation to families and friends — and parents are the proud audience.

Think Critically

Kids explore both sides — the promise and the pitfalls of AI. They'll discuss bias, energy use, economic impact, and the importance of human creativity and judgment.

Pilot Camp — August 17–21, 2026

This is a community-built pilot — not a team project. We're gathering 15 Chicago families to shape AI literacy for the next generation, together. Parents and kids learn side by side, with sessions designed for kids, parents, and families together.
