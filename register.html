Register for the Pilot

Join our pilot community of 15 families in Chicago and help shape AI literacy for the next generation. Parents are welcome to join any day — because this is built by community, not a team.

We'll use this to share camp details and updates.

If you can't attend, your child is still welcome. Parents who can join are encouraged to — the camp is designed for families to learn side by side.

Our camp is designed for ages 8–12 (Grades 2–6). Optional — we'll ask for this if needed.

Privacy Notice

What we collect: Your name, email address, your child's age, and any additional information you voluntarily provide (such as accessibility needs or questions).

Why we collect it: To register your child for the educAItion pilot camp, communicate camp-related details (schedule, location updates, what to bring), and respond to any questions or concerns you share.

How we protect it: Your data is stored securely through Netlify's form service. Only the educAItion community has access to registration information. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.

How long we keep it: Your data is retained for 12 months after the pilot camp concludes, after which it will be securely deleted.

Your rights: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us. You may also withdraw your consent for future communications at any time.

COPPA compliance: This camp is designed for parents and guardians of children ages 8–12. We do not collect information directly from children and do not target our camp to children under 13. Parental consent is required for registration.

Questions?

We're happy to help. Email us at hello@educateducation.com or register above and we'll follow up with all the details.
