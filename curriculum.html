Five Days of Discovery

Each day builds on the last, guiding kids from curiosity to creation. Here's a peek at the journey.

1

What Is AI?

Kids explore what AI actually is through hands-on demos — no jargon, no fear. They'll try AI tools, spot the patterns, and start to understand how machines "learn." Everyone picks a real problem they want to solve by camp's end.

2

How AI Learns

Through games and experiments, kids discover training data, patterns, and prediction. They'll see what bias looks like in practice and learn why the data matters as much as the algorithm.

3

AI in the World

Kids investigate real AI applications — in healthcare, art, climate science, and more. They'll debate the tradeoffs: the promise of AI alongside its costs in energy, bias, and economic disruption. No sugar-coating, no doom.

4

Build & Create

This is the hands-on week. Kids use kid-friendly tools to build their own AI-powered project — a chatbot, an image classifier, a creative generator. They work in small groups, learn by doing, and start preparing their showcase.

5

Showcase Day

Families and friends are invited to watch kids present their projects. Each child explains their problem, their AI solution, and what they learned about the world of AI. A celebration of what kids can create when they understand the tools.

Built for Generations

For Kids

Hands-on exploration: building AI-powered projects, debating tradeoffs, and discovering what machines can and cannot do. Designed for ages 8–12.

👨‍👩‍👧

For Parents

Dedicated sessions to explore AI literacy from the parent's perspective: navigating AI in the home, understanding bias and ethics, and preparing for an AI-driven future.

Family-together activities: creative labs, ethics debates, project building, and the Day 5 showcase where kids present their work to parents and the community.
